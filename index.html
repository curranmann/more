Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pay per call services

What exactly is pay per call marketing?
Pay per call is a performance-based marketing model where you only pay when we deliver a qualified phone call to your business. Unlike traditional advertising where you pay for impressions or clicks, with pay per call you're investing directly in potential customers who are actively seeking your services.
How do you define a "qualified" call?
We define qualified calls as those that meet specific criteria we establish together based on your business needs. Typically, this includes verification that the caller is within your service area, needs a service you provide, and has a legitimate need for immediate towing or roadside assistance. Any calls that don't meet these criteria are not charged to you.
What is the typical cost per call?
The cost per call varies based on your service area, competition level, and the specific qualifications we set for your calls. However, our clients typically see a cost per acquisition that's 30-50% lower than their previous marketing efforts. We're happy to provide a customized quote based on your unique business needs.
Is there a minimum commitment or contract?
We offer flexible arrangements to suit businesses of all sizes. While we do have monthly minimum call packages available, we also offer pay-as-you-go options for smaller operations or those just testing our services. Our standard agreements are month-to-month, so you're never locked into a long-term contract.
How quickly can you start sending calls to my business?
Most clients start receiving calls within 3-5 business days after completing our onboarding process. This includes setting up call tracking, establishing call qualification criteria, and configuring your account in our system. For businesses with unique requirements, it may take slightly longer to customize our campaigns to your specifications.
What if I need to pause or adjust my call volume?
We understand that business capacity can fluctuate. You can adjust your call volume or temporarily pause your campaign with just 24 hours' notice. This flexibility ensures you never receive more calls than you can handle effectively, maintaining service quality and maximizing your ROI.
How do you generate the calls?
We use a multi-channel marketing approach that includes search engine marketing, social media advertising, local SEO, directory listings, and strategic partnerships with roadside assistance networks. Each channel is optimized specifically for towing services and targeted to your service area to maximize call quality and volume.
What reporting will I receive?
You'll have access to a real-time dashboard that shows call volume, call duration, conversion rates, and ROI metrics. We also provide detailed monthly reports with call recordings, geographic distribution analysis, peak time data, and optimization recommendations to continuously improve your results.
